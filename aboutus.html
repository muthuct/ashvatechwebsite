About Us

Incorporated in 2004 in Chennai, India, Ashva Digital Healthcare was set up by a team of energetic young people to provide value-added solutions in the medical imaging IT space. With a strong development team stationed in Chennai, we provide radiology software solutions in India, the USA, the Middle East and South East Asia.

Ashva's picture archiving and communication systems ensure easy, swift processing of laboratory results into DICOM format files. Not only does this reduce the component of human error significantly, it also much simplifies the process of providing editable copies to the doctor, patient, lab technician, consultant specialists, and other authorised personnel.

Our conscious decision to follow the maxim, "simple is powerful", together with our ear-to-the-ground approach to matters related to our core competencies – and of course our high-quality, cost-effective products – ensure that we have over one thousand satisfied customers across geographies and product lines.
